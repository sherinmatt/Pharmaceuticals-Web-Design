Summary: Mouse Weight vs Avg Tumor Volume for X

The goal of this project is to analyze the impact of the four different anti-cancer drugs. Here, I compare, mouse weight vs average volume of tumor, for the following drug regimens: Capomulin, Ramicane, Infubinol and Ceftamin. The main goal is to look at how anti-cancer drug Capomulin fared as compared to other three drugs.

I utilized Matplotlib to combine and analyze the mouse_metadata and study_result csv files. The final conlusion was that Capomulin did have a significant impact when compared to the other three major drugs.
